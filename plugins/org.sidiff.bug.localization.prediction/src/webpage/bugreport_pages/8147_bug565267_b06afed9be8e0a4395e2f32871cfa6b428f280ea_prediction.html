Creation Time 2020-07-16T09:29:50Z
The word boundary detection for subword matching doesn't handle some names with mixed camel case and caps (eg. acronyms) correctly. Examples: * IImportWizard, not matched by "import", should be I[Import]Wizard * HTMLTable, not matched by "table", should be HTML[Table] Correct: * CustomHTMLTable, matched correctly by "table": CustomHTML[Table] -> the word start after capital letters at the beginning of the name is not detected
