Creation Time 2020-05-04T09:54:25Z
ClasspathJrtWithReleaseOption overrides ClasspathJrt.cleanup() but does not call super.cleanup(), instead, it calls super.reset() (that does nothing). ClasspathJrt.cleanup() *would* close external annotations jar, but obviously this is not happening for ClasspathJrtWithReleaseOption. I believe this is a mistake, and we should call super.cleanup().
