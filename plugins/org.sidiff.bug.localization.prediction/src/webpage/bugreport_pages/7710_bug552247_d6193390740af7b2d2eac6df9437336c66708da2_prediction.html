Creation Time 2019-10-19T17:59:54Z
IJavaProject#getModuleDescription() considers the patch-module classpath attribute and may answer the module being patched. This is convenient from the project's perspective, but I've encountered several situations where looking from outside clients are interested only in the module-info defined inside the project. I will add IJavaProject#getOwnModuleDescription() for those clients.
