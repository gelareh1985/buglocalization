Creation Time 2019-08-03T08:29:52Z
When I have a @NonNullByDefailt annotation in package-info.java and I use a constant in the annotation of a class, then the NNBD of package-info.java no longer effects the class. Especially the constants of the class are no longer annotated as NonNull. When I call library methods that require non null I get an error, that the provided constant is not non-null. My workaround ist to annotate the class with NNBD but then I get a errro/warning/info (as configured) about duplicated null annotation. I often use constants in annotations, when it comes to url-paths.
