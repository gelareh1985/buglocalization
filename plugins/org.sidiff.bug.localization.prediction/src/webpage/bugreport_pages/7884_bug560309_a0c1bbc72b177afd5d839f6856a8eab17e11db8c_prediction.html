The subword matcher currently has no backtracking, e.g. the following doesn't match the pattern "addlistener": addListListener() ^ | it can't match 'e' here and breaks Instead, it should backtrack within the pattern and look for the next occurence of "List" and match the following regions: [add]List[Listener]
